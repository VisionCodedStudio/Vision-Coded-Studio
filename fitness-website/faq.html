Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about memberships, training, and our gym services.

What are your gym hours?

We are open Monday – Friday from 5am to 10pm and Saturday – Sunday from 5am to 8pm.

Do I need a membership to train?

Yes. Our gym operates on a membership model. Membership plans provide full access to equipment, training programs, and classes depending on your plan.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes. Certified personal trainers are available for one-on-one sessions as well as customized fitness programs.

Can beginners join the gym?

Absolutely. Our trainers work with beginners and experienced athletes. Every member can receive a fitness assessment to get started.

Do you offer group classes?

Yes. Group training classes are included with select memberships and focus on strength, conditioning, and functional training.

Where are you located?

Iron Pulse Fitness is located at 742 S Hill Street, Los Angeles, CA 90014.
